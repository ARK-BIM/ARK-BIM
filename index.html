ARK-BIM

a custom cloud BIM platform for archaeology

What is ARK-BIM?

ARK-BIM is an ad-hoc web platform developed on the basis of BIMData (BIMData.io) thanks to FOSS development tools (JavaScript, VueJS, XEOKIT and HTML) and plugin implementation

Open Source

ARK-BIM is based on powerfull BIMData (BIMData.io) and XEOGL (xeolabs.com) open source codes. Thanks to FOSS development tools via JavaScript, VueJS, XEOKIT and HTML a custom viewer and platform can be created including ad-hoc plugins. ARK-BIM codes are free to be shared and implemented

For Cultural Heritage

ARK-BIM was designed for sharing and managing HBIM projects related to Archaeology and Cultural Heritage assets. In fact, actual web and client BIM platform have been designed for AEC (Architecture, Engineering & Construction) purposes, and they rarely can be adapted to historical peculiarities.

Ad-hoc features

In addition to all BIMData features, ARK-BIM includes different plugins like: isolate elements; hotspot model; stratigraphic unit annotation; measurements center, 2D-3D switch and other important functionalities designed for Archaeological domain and Cultural Heritage. Its functionalities will be constantly implemented
